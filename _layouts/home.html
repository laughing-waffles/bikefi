What is an e-bike loan?

E-bike loans are an easy way to finance the purchase of your bike. Because a bike isn’t registered with the government like a car, the loan is typically considered an “unsecured personal loan”.

This means lenders can’t use the bike itself as collateral, so you’ll likely see higher interest rates than purchasing a car. The good news is that since most e-bikes are cheaper than cars, the actual dollar amount you’ll pay per month will be significantly lower.
