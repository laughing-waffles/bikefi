## Kali Chakra Child Artist Series Helmet

### Grom-Approved

The Kali Chakra Child Artist Series helmet brings Kali’s dedication to
protection to growing riders. With extended back of the head coverage
providing additional peace of mind, and Kali’s Composite Fusion™ technology
reducing helmet weight and increasing impact absorption. The Chakra features a
simple and reliable Dial-Fit retention system on size small, with an elastic
band design on XS sizes to make it easier on the youngest riders. In creating
this limited edition Artist Series Chakra helmet, Kali partnered with self-
employed artists facing hardships in the face of the COVID-19 pandemic, with
Kali’s profits from the sale of this helmet going to the artists. The Kali
Chakra Child keeps your young shredder comfortably protected and looking
sharp, too!

### Features

  * **Limited edition Artist Series** helmets are designed by self-employed artists, with all of Kali’s profits going straight to the artists themselves
  * **Designed for riders 1 year old and up** refer to sizing chart below to confirm sizing
  * **Composite Fusion™ technology** bonds the helmet shell and foam together, providing a lighter weight, more durable and protective helmet
  * **Designed by artist Cori Dantini** from Pullman, Washington
  * **Integrated visor** offers protection from sun and branches
  * **Note:** XS size features an elastic band fit system for easier use for the youngest riders
  * Lifetime Crash Replacement Guidelines
